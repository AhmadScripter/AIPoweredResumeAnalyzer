About Resume Analyzer

An AI powered platform that helps you understand how well your resume matches a job description.

What We Do

Resume Analyzer allows users to upload their resumes and compare them with job descriptions. The system identifies matching skills, missing skills, and provides a clear match percentage to help users improve their resumes.

Why Use Resume Analyzer

  • Analyze resume against any job description
  • See matched and missing skills instantly
  • Get AI based improvement suggestions
  • Download analysis reports as PDF
  • Track your past analysis history

Our Mission

Our mission is to help job seekers present their skills effectively and increase their chances of getting shortlisted by recruiters.

Built for students, fresh graduates, and professionals who want to stand out in the job market.
